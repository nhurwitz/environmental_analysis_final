Who is Lotte?

Lotte, a 34-year-old Danish woman living in the Copenhagen suburb of Frederiksburg, is always on the move. In 2008, Lotte kickstarted her own small business, opening up a storefront near Copenhagen's city center. Though her business thrived during its early years, Lotte started to gain weight and struggled to find time for exercise. In late 2010, she decided to make a change.
